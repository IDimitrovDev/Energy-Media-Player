## Enable Dolby Vision Playback

Energy Media Player supports Dolby Vision media encoded in profiles 5, 8, and 9. To enable playback, two extensions must be installed:

- **Dolby Vision Extensions** — available from the [Microsoft Store](ms-windows-store://pdp/?ProductId=9pltg1lwphlf)  
- **HEVC Video Extensions** — available in two forms:
  1. **Paid version** from the [Microsoft Store](ms-windows-store://pdp/?ProductId=9NMZLZ57R3T7)  
  2. **Free OEM version** (delisted from the Store but still available on internet by searching for "HEVC Video Extensions from Device Manufacturer")

Once both extensions are installed:

1. Open Energy Media Player.
2. Navigate to **Settings → Video Settings**.
3. Enable **Allow Dolby Vision decoding**.
![alt text](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/IDimitrovDev/Energy-Media-Player/refs/heads/master/Assets/Allow%20Dolby%20Vision%20Decoding.png)
When playing supported Dolby Vision media:

- On **Dolby Vision licensed devices**, the Dolby Vision logo appears briefly at playback start.
- On **non-licensed devices**, decoding still occurs, but the logo is not shown.

To verify Dolby Vision playback, open the context menu -> Video -> Decode mode and.
